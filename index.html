Don Toliver

Don Toliver is an American rapper born in Houston, Texas. He is 29 years old. He has dropped 3 studio albums: Donny Womack,
Life of a DON, and LoveSick. His popular songs include No Idea, After Party, Cardigan, OUT WEST, Lemonade, and Private Landing.
The general public first gave him recognition from his feature on 'CAN'T SAY' by popular rapper Travis Scott.
